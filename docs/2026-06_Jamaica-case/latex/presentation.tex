    {\fontsize{5pt}{6pt}\selectfont

Shaded bands show the 25th--75th percentile within each income group (advanced economies, emerging markets, low-income) and the lines are the group medians; the thick blue line is Jamaica. Circles mark the 2023 values (the reference year for the calibration) on each group median and on Jamaica; only Jamaica's value is labelled. A larger gap means more room for efficiency-led output gains: Jamaica's infrastructure gap (0.14) lies well below every income group, whereas its health and education gaps sit around the upper part of the group ranges. \\
